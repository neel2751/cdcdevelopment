Company Criteria for Risk Management

we understand the importance of safeguarding your investment and ensuring your peace of mind. That's why we prioritize a comprehensive proactive risk management approach that goes beyond simply reacting to problems.

Thorough Tenant Screening
This involves a comprehensive screening process that verifies credit history, employment, income, and rental history, including references and background checks.
Inspection & Maintenance
Proactive maintenance is key. Regular inspections identify and address minor issues before they become major problems that could lead to property damage, injuries, or lawsuits.
Lease Agreements
Well-defined lease agreements that outline tenant responsibilities, rent payment terms, and consequences for violations minimize misunderstandings and legal disputes.
Robust Insurance Coverage
Adequate property and liability insurance protects the company from financial losses due to fire, theft, vandalism, or tenant-related issues.
Communication and Training
Educate staff on potential risks, proper procedures, and emergency protocols. This empowers them to identify and address risks effectively.
Record Documentation
Maintain detailed records of tenant applications, lease agreements, logs, and communication with tenants. This is crucial for resolving disputes and demonstrating compliance with regulations.
